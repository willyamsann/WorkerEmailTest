Hispanic Heritage Month Experiences

This year the DEI Committee will host three experiences during Hispanic Heritage Month. The goal of these experiences is to broaden our knowledge of the Hispanic culture, recognize our teammates who are Hispanic or LatinX (the broader term for the Latino and Latina people) and create excitement about learning useful Spanish phrases. Join us this week for the virtual Spanish Teaching Session. Registration is still available.

Spanish Teaching Session

September 22 from 10:00-11:00am
Virtual

Interested in learning a few key Spanish phrases that you can use at work? During this session, you will have the opportunity to do just that. Get ready for a fun immersive experience where you will learn some common phrases you can implement into everyday practice as an insurance professional.

Instructors: Vanessa Ortiz and Monica Bew
